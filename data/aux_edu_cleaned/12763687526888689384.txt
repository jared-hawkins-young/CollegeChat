Van Landingham Rd. closed 7/22/11 & 7/23/11

Facilities Management has just informed PaTS that Van Landingham Road, between John Kirk Dr. and Mary Alexander Rd., will be closed on Friday, July 22nd and Saturday, July 23rd for final paving and striping. On Friday 7/22 and Saturday 7/23, the only access in and out of East Decks 2 and 3 will be via the West-bound lane of University City Blvd.
. East Deck 1 Visitor and Faculty/Staff parking will not be impacted. Parking lots 5 and 5A may be accessed from Cameron Blvd. and Martin Village Rd.
. Parking Lots 4 and 4A will not be available on either Friday or Saturday.
