Get graduation attire at the Spring Commencement Fair



Come to graduation prepared and looking your best by visiting the Spring 2023 Commencement Fair. The commencement fair is your one-stop shop for commencement attire, cap and gown, regalia, class rings, diploma frames and more.

The event will take place on April 5-6, 9 a.m.- 6 p.m., in Barnes & Noble Charlotte in the Popp Martin Student Union. 

You do not need to pre-order your cap and gown, but you must present your 49er ID at pickup. For more information on pre-ordering attire and gear, visit the Charlotte commencement cap and gown page.
