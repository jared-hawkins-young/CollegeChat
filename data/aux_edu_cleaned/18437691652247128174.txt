Dining Services

UNC Charlotte offers a wide variety of dining options in different locations across campus. From buffet-style dining at Social 704 and SoVi to retail dining at our over 20 locations, you’ll find something that suits your taste, budget and food preferences. We offer a number of flexible meal plans suitable for resident students and commuters.
