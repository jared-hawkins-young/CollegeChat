More Niner Course Pack benefits to get excited about

In addition to saving students an average of 30 to 50 percent on the cost of textbooks, the Niner Course Pack program will offer students who stay opted in to the program an exclusive campus store savings pass!

Students who receive their course materials through the program will get an exclusive VIP card when they pick up their order at the campus store. With this VIP card, students will get discounts on important items to help them succeed this term.
