Passport Services

All your passport needs in one central location

We are an authorized U.S. passport acceptance facility offering appointment-based services. Come see us to apply for a passport. Not only do we serve the UNC Charlotte campus, we are open to the public and our neighbors in the Charlotte metro area. Open appointments are available Monday through Friday, 8:30 a.m. – 2 p.m. and walk-ins 2:15 p.m. – 3:15 p.m. for Students, Faculty & Staff ONLY. Passports are processed at Union Station located in the Popp Martin Student Union.
