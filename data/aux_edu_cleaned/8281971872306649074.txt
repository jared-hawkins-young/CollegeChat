Bring a Guest to Bistro for Free

For a limited time, dining patrons can bring a guest to Bistro for free! Take advantage of the offer on Wednesdays between 11am and 11:45am. 

Bistro is an elevated buffet-style restaurant located on the second floor of the Popp Martin Student Union, next to Social 704. Enjoy appetizers, garden-fresh salads, generous sandwiches, and enticing desserts.

It’s the perfect spot for your next lunch-time date or work meeting.

Find Bistro hours on the Dine on Campus website or Dine on Campus mobile app.
