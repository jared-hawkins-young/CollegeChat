Campus closings for Fourth of July week

Dining

Crown Commons and Market on Craver will be closed on Friday, July 3 and Saturday, July 4 in observance of U.S. Independence Day. Crown Commons will reopen on Sunday, July 5 at 11 a.m.

Market on Craver will also be closed on Sunday, July 5 and will reopen on Monday, July 6 at 10 a.m.

For more information about dining hours, please visit Dine on Campus.

Parking and Transportation

Niner Transit will not run on Friday, July 3. Weekend service on July 4 and July 5 will be available from 9 a.m. to 9 p.m.

The PaTS front counter and the Communication Center will be closed on Friday, July 3.

Barnes and Noble Charlotte

Barnes and Noble Charlotte will be closed on Friday, July 3.

Mail

Mail and Package Services and Union Station will be closed on Friday, July 3.

REPROS

REPROS will not accept online or phone orders on July 3.

For updated hours, please visit the Auxiliary Services hours page. Have a happy and safe Fourth of July weekend!
